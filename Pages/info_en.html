LGSP Intercontinental Youth Cup – Portugal’s Largest Under-12 Football Tournament The LGSP Intercontinental Youth Cup is an international under-12 football tournament that has established itself as one of Europe’s largest and most prestigious youth football tournaments, held in the Porto region. Created with the aim of taking youth football to a new level, this event brings together elite national and international teams, providing a high-level competition for young talents. Organised by LGSP Sports and IFT – Individual Football Training, with the support of the Municipal of Gondomar and Sport Clube Rio Tinto, the tournament will reach its 4th edition in 2025, taking place on 17, 18 and 19 April 2025. This edition consolidates the event as a benchmark in under-12 football tournaments in northern Portugal.

A leading international Under-12 football tournament

At a time when Portuguese football is recognised worldwide for the quality of its players, the LGSP Intercontinental Youth Cup has been launched to address the shortage of high-level youth tournaments in the northern region. The event stands out for inviting some of the best European clubs, creating a competitive environment that prepares young players for the challenges of high-performance football. This international Under-12 tournament is designed to develop not only sporting performance, but also the human values that make football a school of life.

More than just a youth football tournament

The LGSP Intercontinental Youth Cup is much more than a simple youth football tournament. It is an event that promotes:

  • Sportsmanship and fair play
  • Interaction between athletes from different countries
  • Education, respect and team spirit
  • Unique experiences in youth football
    • The organisation believes that these values are fundamental to the development of young players and make this tournament one of the best under-12 football tournaments in Europe.

    The Future of Football is in Porto

    With an excellent organisation, quality infrastructure and an international vision, the LGSP Intercontinental Youth Cup positions itself as the largest under-12 football tournament held in northern Portugal, attracting clubs, athletes, coaches and families from all over the world. If you are looking for an international under-12 football tournament, competitive, developmental and recognised, the LGSP Intercontinental Youth Cup is the ideal stage for the talents of the future.
