Welcome to the LIYC Cards

LIYC Cards bring the professional football experience to the LGSP Intercontinental Youth Cup. Inspired by the iconic FIFA-style player cards, each participant receives a personalized digital card showcasing their name, team, position, photo, and performance ratings. It’s more than just a keepsake — it’s a moment of recognition, giving young athletes the spotlight they deserve and a memory they’ll carry long after the tournament ends.
