Lufthansa Ground Services Portugal (LGSP) is a wholly-owned subsidiary of the Deutsche Lufthansa AG group, with its operational hub based in Porto, and is a sister company to, amongst others, Lufthansa Airlines, Swiss Airlines and Lufthansa Cargo. LGSP invests in other solid partnerships, focusing on the core competencies of its four operational areas: Stopover Representation, Administration and Supervision, Passenger Handling, Ramp Supervision and Ticketing, whenever strategically and economically advisable. LGSP’s services include the organisation of national and international events in the sporting, cultural and gastronomic sectors, among others, thereby extending the already recognised quality of its services and complementing its ground-based offering. As a subsidiary of the Deutsche Lufthansa AG group, the company places great importance on quality and innovation, safety and reliability. Sporting events are organised under the LGSP Sports and Events brand.

LGSP Sports and Events

LGSP Sports and Events is dedicated to creating comprehensive, high-quality sporting experiences, bringing together in one place everything that athletes, teams and organisations need to develop. From specialised training programmes to internationally renowned tournaments, we offer environments where performance, learning and competitive spirit come together. Our projects include sports exchanges, intensive training camps and team-building activities designed to promote not only performance, but also team cohesion and personal development. To ensure ideal conditions, we provide quality accommodation, high-performance training centres, transport services and medical support — elements that guarantee a safe, integrated experience focused on each team’s objectives. At LGSP Sports, we believe that sport is a global bridge — and we work every day to create opportunities that inspire talent, build character and bring cultures together.

    • LGSP Porto Corporate League

    The LGSP Porto Corporate League is a corporate 7-a-side football league in Portugal, established in November 2025, with the aim of promoting team spirit, networking and the creation of business connections through sport. Based in Porto, this competition was developed specifically for companies seeking an organised, competitive and friendly sporting experience. The league stems from the growing need for corporate football that goes beyond the game itself, serving as a genuine platform for corporate team building, business socialising and professional networking outside the workplace. The LGSP Porto Corporate League is committed to an accessible, professional format focused on fair play, providing a positive experience for all participants.
